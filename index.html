Your Complete 7-Day Gym Workout Plan By Aryan

Optimized for your Chest/Tri, Back/Bi, Legs/Shoulders split with a 1 hour 15 minute gym session goal.

Important Notes for Your 75-Minute Session:

  • Warm-up (5-10 mins): Essential before every workout.
  • Strength Training (55-60 mins): Focus on compound movements.
  • Cool-down & Stretching (5-10 mins): Crucial for recovery and flexibility.
  • Rep Range: Generally 8-12 reps for muscle growth. Adjust weight to hit this range.
  • Sets: 3-4 sets per exercise.
  • Rest: 60-90 seconds between sets for strength/hypertrophy.
  • Progressive Overload: Aim to gradually lift heavier or do more reps/sets over time.
  • Listen to Your Body: If something hurts, stop.
  • Hydration & Nutrition: Drink plenty of water and fuel your body well.

Warm-up & Cool-down Flow:

  • General Warm-up (5 minutes): Light cardio, dynamic arm circles, leg swings, torso twists (applies to every day).
  • Specific Dynamic Warm-up (2-3 minutes): Focus on the muscles you're about to train (detailed below for each day).
  • Workout (55-60 minutes).
  • Static Stretching (5-10 minutes): Cool-down, holding stretches (detailed below for each day).

Your 7-Day Workout Schedule with Specific Warm-ups & Stretches

Monday: Chest & Triceps

Dynamic Warm-up (5-10 minutes)

  • General Cardio: 5 mins on treadmill, elliptical, or jumping jacks.
  • Arm Circles: Forward & Backward, gradually increasing range. (Search GIF)
  • Band Pull-Aparts: With a light resistance band, focus on warming up the rear delts and upper back to stabilize shoulders. (Search GIF)
  • Scapular Push-ups/Retractions: Focus on shoulder blade movement. (Search GIF)
  • Light Push-ups or Chest Flyes (no weight): 10-15 reps to activate chest. (Search GIF)
  • Workout (Approx. 55-60 mins)
  • 1. Barbell Bench Press 4 sets x 8-12 reps (Search GIF)
  • 2. Incline Dumbbell Press 3 sets x 10-15 reps (Search GIF)
  • 3. Cable Flyes (Mid-cable) 3 sets x 12-15 reps (Search GIF)
  • 4. Close-Grip Bench Press OR Dips (Assisted if needed) 3 sets x 8-12 reps (Search GIF)
  • 5. Overhead Dumbbell Triceps Extension 3 sets x 10-15 reps (Search GIF)
  • 6. Triceps Pushdowns (Rope or Bar) 3 sets x 12-15 reps (Search GIF)

Static Stretching (5-10 minutes - Cool-down)

  • Hold each stretch for 20-30 seconds. Do not bounce.
  • Pec Stretch (Doorway or Wall): Stand in a doorway, place forearms on the frame, lean forward. (Search GIF)
  • Overhead Triceps Stretch: Arm over head, use other hand to gently pull elbow down. (Search GIF)
  • Child's Pose: For overall upper body and back relaxation. (Search GIF)
